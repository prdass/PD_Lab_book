## Methods

### Per reaction (50uL total):
DNA mix (25uL)
- 17uL water
- 1uL dNTPs
- 1uL forward primer
- 1uL reverse primer
- 5uL template DNA (should be approx 5ng/uL)

Enzyme mix (25uL)
- 19.25uL water
- 5uL Buffer
- 0.75uL enzyme

Make up each mix separately using filter tips and combine 25uL of each mix in the 0.2mL PCR tube.
